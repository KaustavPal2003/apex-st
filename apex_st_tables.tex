\textbf{Module} &
  \textbf{Bug description} &
  \textbf{Fix} &
  \textbf{Before} &
  \textbf{After} \\
\midrule

\texttt{sprint3\_gat.py} \newline (GAT correlation graph) &
  Correlation matrix built from raw future price levels (\texttt{y\_reg}),
  inflating correlations via shared bull-market drift rather than
  genuine daily co-movement. Node features also derived from the
  forward-shifted target array, leaking future returns into inputs. &
  Read daily log-returns directly from \texttt{data/\{SYM\}.csv};
  never use \texttt{y\_reg} for graph construction. &
  80--99\% &
  47--53\% \\

\addlinespace
\texttt{apex\_feature\_ engineering.py} \newline (\texttt{y\_reg} target) &
  \texttt{y\_reg} stored raw future closing price (level) instead of
  a 30-day log-return. Caused GRU to train on a non-stationary,
  scale-dependent target; also caused Training loss explosion
  ($\times$600{,}000) when normalisation was later removed. &
  Replace \texttt{y\_reg.append(future\_price)} with
  \texttt{y\_reg.append(log(future/curr))}. &
  N/A \newline (loss = $1.7 \times 10^5$) &
  Loss $\approx 0.27$ \\

\addlinespace
\texttt{sprint3\_finbert.py} \newline (sentiment alignment) &
  Real headlines loaded correctly but then scrambled to wrong
  trading days via even-chunking: chunk $i$ mapped to trading
  day $i$ rather than to the actual calendar date of the headlines. &
  Wire \texttt{aggregate\_daily()} using \texttt{\{SYM\}\_apex\_ dates\_*.npy}
  arrays carrying true decision-day timestamps. &
  49.95\% \newline (broken) &
  49.27\% \newline (honest) \\

\addlinespace
\texttt{stock\_screener.py} \newline (fundamentals gate) &
  \texttt{numpy.bool\_ is not False} identity check always evaluated
  \texttt{True}, silently passing every symbol through Stage~2
  regardless of whether it met growth/cap-tier criteria. &
  Wrap result in \texttt{bool()} before identity comparison. &
  All symbols pass &
  Correct filtering \\
